Global CO2 emissions

In this chart we see the growth of global emissions from the mid-19th century through to today. Carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions in 2020 amounted to approximately 35 billion tons, an increase of more than 150 times compared to 1850.

You can see the detailed information by mouse over dots. Also, if you click on CO2 emissions per fuel type button, you can check the CO2 emissions from each fossil fuel.
